Our restaurant

Dining & Drinks

During the summer, outdoor dining at the Restaurant L’Amour dans le Four is a true pleasure. Our restaurant’s culinary style is further enhanced by the mild climate and relaxing outdoor atmosphere at the Hotel Anis: an ample terrace shaded by a large ficus and overlooking and overlooking a garden enveloped by a thousand different fragrances.

Our pool

Pool

You don’t need to be a luxury mega-resort to have a swimming pool with a world-class view. Case in point is this little boutique spot nestled up in the mountains around Lake Atitlan. The infinity pool at the 15-guest resort sits over the water like one might at a villa on Lake Como, at a fraction of the price. So if you find yourself in Central America, make a point to take a refreshing dip here.
